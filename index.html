Day in the Bright Network Festival

Read time: 10 minutes

The Bright Network Festival is a celebration of career opportunities, professional growth, and networking. I had the privilege of attending this year, and it was nothing short of remarkable. From the moment I walked into the venue, I was greeted by the bustling energy of students, professionals, and recruiters all excited to connect, learn, and explore new possibilities. The day kicked off with a keynote speech by industry leaders, sharing insights on the future of various sectors including finance, technology, and consulting. The speakers’ knowledge and passion were truly inspiring, providing a glimpse into the trends shaping tomorrow's workforce. This opening session set the tone for the rest of the day, encouraging attendees to dream big and pursue their goals. As the day unfolded, there were countless opportunities to interact with top employers. Company booths were set up, each offering information about their internships, graduate schemes, and entry-level positions. From giants like Google and Deloitte to innovative startups, there was something for everyone. Attendees like myself had the chance to ask direct questions, submit resumes, and even get career advice from HR representatives and industry experts. Networking was a key focus of the event, and there were several dedicated sessions where students could mingle with professionals. The casual yet professional atmosphere made it easy to strike up conversations and form connections. In one session, I had an engaging conversation with a manager from PwC who offered invaluable advice about navigating the application process and standing out in a competitive job market. These interactions were one of the festival's highlights, giving attendees like me the chance to form real connections that could potentially lead to future career opportunities. In addition to networking, the festival also featured several career workshops, covering topics such as interview techniques, CV writing, and building a professional online presence. These workshops were interactive and practical, providing us with tools we could apply immediately in our job search. One of the most exciting parts of the day was the panel discussion featuring young professionals who had recently graduated and landed jobs through the Bright Network. Hearing their stories—full of challenges, perseverance, and success—was incredibly motivating. It was reassuring to know that the journey to a successful career is not always straightforward, but with the right mindset and resources, anything is possible. By the end of the day, I walked away with a wealth of knowledge, a handful of new contacts, and a renewed sense of confidence in my career prospects. The Bright Network Festival isn’t just a day of career exploration—it’s a platform for growth, learning, and building a professional network that can open doors to endless opportunities. I highly recommend attending if you're looking to make meaningful strides in your professional journey.

Rayan Gohir 2024

Networking at the Festival

Read time: 10 minutes

Networking at the Bright Network Festival was an incredible experience, offering attendees the opportunity to connect with professionals and peers in a meaningful way. As soon as I arrived, it was clear that the festival was designed to foster genuine connections between aspiring graduates and industry leaders. One of the standout features of the event was the accessibility of professionals from various companies. Whether you were interested in finance, consulting, or tech, representatives from top firms were readily available to engage in conversations. These interactions weren’t just about exchanging pleasantries; they were opportunities to ask questions, learn about company cultures, and gain insights into how different organizations operate. It was an invaluable way to get a feel for the companies that best align with my career goals. The networking sessions were well-structured, making it easy to navigate from one conversation to the next. In addition to the company booths, there were speed-networking sessions that provided a more formal opportunity to engage with professionals. I found this particularly helpful because it ensured that I had direct face time with recruiters and industry experts, even at a busy event. What made the networking at the festival truly impactful was the level of advice and guidance shared. I remember having an insightful conversation with a recruiter from a major consulting firm. Not only did she provide me with tips on tailoring my CV for consulting roles, but she also shared her own experiences, which gave me a better understanding of what to expect in the recruitment process. Another benefit of attending the festival was the chance to network with fellow attendees. I met students and graduates from different universities, each with unique perspectives and career aspirations. It was inspiring to hear about their journeys, and in many cases, I gained valuable insights from their experiences applying for similar roles. These peer connections have the potential to grow into professional relationships as we all progress in our careers. Overall, the networking opportunities at the Bright Network Festival went beyond just exchanging business cards. It was about building relationships, learning from others, and gaining the confidence to take the next step in my career. Whether you’re looking to make industry connections or simply learn more about potential career paths, the festival is an excellent platform to grow your professional network.

Hashim Hussain 2024

Students Thoughts

Read time: 10 minutes

Attending the Bright Network Festival was an enriching experience for many students, offering both personal and professional growth. The festival brought together a diverse group of attendees, each with unique insights into the event. One student shared, "I found the event extremely valuable for career exploration. Meeting with representatives from leading companies allowed me to get a clearer sense of what employers are looking for and how to tailor my applications accordingly." This sentiment was echoed by many, as the festival provided a rare opportunity to directly interact with industry professionals in an approachable environment. Another attendee emphasized the importance of networking, stating, "What stood out to me was the accessibility of the recruiters. It wasn’t just about collecting business cards, but engaging in meaningful conversations. I walked away feeling much more confident about the next steps in my career." These face-to-face interactions were a highlight, enabling students to make lasting impressions and build connections that could prove vital in their job search. Several students appreciated the festival’s focus on personal development. One attendee remarked, "The workshops were incredibly useful, particularly the sessions on interview techniques and CV writing. I left with actionable advice that I can apply as I start applying for graduate roles." The event offered practical guidance that catered to students at various stages of their career journey, from those still exploring options to those ready to apply for jobs. In addition to the professional benefits, the social aspect of the event also stood out. Students from universities across the country came together, creating a sense of community. "I made new friends who are on the same path as me. It was inspiring to hear their stories and share our experiences," one student reflected. Overall, the Bright Network Festival left a lasting impact on its student attendees. The combination of networking opportunities, skill-building workshops, and a supportive environment made it a must-attend event for any student serious about their career.

Waleed Rehman 2024
